Air Force Bal Bharati School

25 October - 27 October

The Principal's Message

“The Youth of today are the Leaders of tomorrow”. We are delighted to extend a warm welcome to all participants as we commence the second session of the AFBBS Model United Nations Conference, Transcendence MUN. Consistent efforts have been made at various levels to address all-encompassing issues of poverty, hunger, human rights violation, and environmental degradation.

In light of these complex factors, there arises an urgent need to cultivate the talents of our brightest minds, empowering them to address these pressing global issues. Emulating the established international program format, Transcendence Model United Nations aims to offer an avenue for numerous young individuals to confront these challenges. By adhering to requisite protocols and comprehending the intricacies, they can contribute to making our shared world safer and more prosperous.

As we embrace each delegate within this program, I hold the hope that the investment of efforts, made by educational institutions will yield substantial returns. The journey of preparation is underway, and it would be a testament to the dedication of educators if we witness concrete outcomes stemming from their guidance and training.

May the conference be met with resounding success.


Sunita Gupta

Principal

The Faculaty Advisor's Message

At Air Force Bal Bharati School, the Model United Nations program has seamlessly integrated into the fabric of the learning process, benefiting both students and teachers. Personally, I have garnered significant insights through this journey. Students have the freedom to engage as delegates, ambassadors, chairpersons, or organizers, showcasing their versatility. As the Faculty Advisor, observing students shoulder responsibilities, transition into new roles, and exert immense effort to achieve their objectives has been a gratifying experience. Witnessing their independent completion of remarkable tasks with minimal guidance fills me with pride and admiration.

The escalating significance of United Nations simulations is conspicuous in our globalized world, given the plethora of international issues and crises. Such platforms not only facilitate a comprehensive grasp of our multinational political mechanism but also nurture responsible future citizens. These individuals will possess a profound awareness of worldwide concerns, empowered with the knowledge to proactively contribute to the betterment of our planet. The AFBBS MUNC experience substantially hones students' research, debate, public speaking, teamwork, and presentation skills.

As we anticipate the second edition of the Air Force Bal Bharati School Model United Nations Conference (AFBBS MUNC), it is our aspiration that all participants find their voices and express their viewpoints ardently. The aim is to foster their intellectual capacities, enabling them to perceive matters with increased profundity. I eagerly anticipate orchestrating the enriching journey during the second edition of the Air Force Bal Bharati School Model United Nations.


Amita Sharad Kaushik

Faculty Advisor

The Secretary-General's Message

In our capacity as the Secretary-General of Transcendence Model United Nations 2023, it is an esteemed privilege to extend a warm welcome to you all, marking the commencement of the second edition of an event that has masterfully showcased the organizational prowess of Air ForceBal Bharati School, Lodi Road students. This occasion marks yet another endeavor to offer participants a distinctive and memorable experience, upholding the time-honored tradition of an academically-driven conference. This year, we have chosen 'Creating The Distinction' as our conference theme, a choice that embodies our commitment to fostering an enriching and enlightening atmosphere.

Within my Secretariat lies a wealth of expertise poised to guide participants on an expedition that will profoundly expand their understanding of international diplomacy, diplomatic relations, and global politics. We assure every delegate that the enhancements to this year's conference have been meticulously designed to cultivate an environment of thoughtful discourse, spirited debate, skillful negotiation, and meaningful compromise, thereby ensuring a truly holistic experience for all involved.

The exhaustive efforts invested by each member of the Secretariat, the Directorate, and the Organizing Committee in orchestrating this event are truly commendable. This endeavor brings together over 500 delegates from esteemed institutions spanning the globe. By inviting students to step into the shoes of diplomats from various nations, representatives of diverse organizations, and ministers overseeing pivotal portfolios, we provide a platform for the next generation of potential leaders to forge concrete, enduring solutions to the myriad challenges that confront our world.

Guided by the belief that "The youth of today is the future of tomorrow," we envision a landscape encompassing nine distinct committees. These committees range from the conventional United Nations bodies to the unconventional, including the All India Political Parties Meet and the highly anticipated Ad-Hoc Committee of the Secretary-General—trademarks of Air Force Bal Bharati School MUN Conference. Our objective this year is to foster superlative debate, thoughtful deliberation, and a profound grasp of the intricacies that define Model United Nations activities. Our committees, undoubtedly, stand apart. Embracing the tenet that our future resides within the vigor of youth, I urge each participant to seize this opportunity for personal growth and enhancement.

As we move forward, may the most accomplished prevail and the most insightful inspire. The Secretariat and us eagerly anticipate the pleasure of hosting you at our splendid campus for this enriching three-day conference.


Soumil Srivastava & Shaambhavi Narayan

Co-Secretary Generals
