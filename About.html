ABOUT AIR FORCE BAL BHARATI SCHOOL

Starting as makeshift wartime barracks on July 15th, 1955, the Air Force Bal Bharati School has evolved into a prominent establishment, delivering exceptional education to the offspring of air force personnel. Commencing with a modest enrollment of 116 students and a dedicated teaching staff of 6, the school has undergone remarkable expansion and advancement. Today, it stands proudly as a comprehensive Senior Secondary institution, addressing the educational requirements of almost 4000 students. Affiliated with the CBSE (Central Board of Secondary Education), the school closely adheres to the guidelines stipulated by the Delhi School Education Act and Rules (DSEAR) of 1973. Functioning as a recognized and self-sustained private school, it enjoys the esteemed endorsement of the Indian Air Force Educational and Cultural Society.

Located on the enchanting Lodi Road surrounded by a canopy of trees, AFBBS serves as an impressive landmark, encompassing a vast area of 7.96 acres. Over time, the school has meticulously developed state-of-the-art infrastructure, presenting abundant academic and extracurricular prospects spanning arts, science, and sports.

'Soar to Achieve' This mantra propels the institution to persistently pursue its vision of cultivating inventive, resilient, eco-conscious, and socially adept individuals within a diverse and nurturing educational milieu.

Leading the school is Mrs. Sunita Gupta, the esteemed Principal and distinguished recipient of the 2018 State Award. Serving as a pillar of the institution, she staunchly advocates for unearthing each student's potential and authentic passions. Driven by an unwavering commitment to holistic growth, the school endeavors to empower students with the necessary skills to reach great heights while embracing their cultural heritage and upholding cherished traditions.

ABOUT TRANSCENDENCE MODEL UNITED NATIONS

Model United Nations (MUN) constitutes an engaging endeavor wherein students actively participate as delegates, stepping into the shoes of diplomats representing the nations they are assigned. In these academic simulations, they replicate authentic UN bodies and other global forums in the realm of international relations. Covering a wide spectrum of topics—ranging from 'Cyber Security' to 'Addressing the Challenge of Islamophobia'—that reverberate across the international community, MUN serves as an educational platform. Its purpose is to enlighten participants about current events, global affairs, and the United Nations' agenda, all while expanding their grasp of international politics, diplomacy, and the imperative for harmonious coexistence. Leveraging negotiation and deliberation skills, students collaboratively formulate solutions to counteract the diverse array of challenges that confront our world today.

Regular society gatherings facilitate discussions and debates on matters of global significance, mirroring the protocols and practices upheld by the United Nations.

At AFBBS MUNC, we are resolute in our aspiration to uphold the legacy of an academically oriented and intellectually enriching encounter for each delegate, building upon the remarkable standards set by the eight highly successful preceding editions. Our aim is to foster an environment that actively spurs delegates to explore and dissect crucial facets of international relations. By doing so, they gain a nuanced perspective on worldwide issues, engage in multifaceted dialogues, and nurture their expertise in diplomacy and negotiation. As they assume the coveted roles of diplomats hailing from diverse nations, the conference offers a unique platform for emerging leaders of our generation. Through collaborative efforts, they endeavor to craft enduring solutions to the array of challenges that our world grapples with, all in the pursuit of a brighter tomorrow.

At AFBBS Model United Nations, we perceive it as our responsibility to cultivate delegates capable of evolving into extraordinary diplomats and tomorrow's trailblazing leaders. Our objective is to curate an environment that sparks critical thought, ignites debates, encourages thorough deliberation, hones negotiation skills, and fosters discussions grounded in the intricate tapestry of international diplomacy.
